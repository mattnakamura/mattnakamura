## 🔬 About Me

I'm a PhD researcher in the [Nanosystems Lab](https://github.com/nanosystemslab) at the University of Hawaiʻi at Mānoa, working at the intersection of **computational mechanics**, **experimental materials characterization**, and **laboratory automation**. My work spans peridynamics-based fracture simulation and topology optimization, computer-vision-driven mechanical testing, and building the Python tooling that drives our lab's instruments and analysis pipelines.

- 🧮 **Simulation:** peridynamics, FEA, topology optimization (Peridigm, FEniCSx)
- 🤖 **Automation:** Python drivers for syringe pumps, PID controllers, valves, flow/pressure meters, and multimeters
- 👁️ **Vision:** automated deformation tracking for mechanical tests
- 📊 **Analysis:** nanoindentation, buckling, creep, and materials characterization pipelines
